🌼 What Happens in a Stress Management or Life Skills Session?

🧠 Understanding Stress

Learn what stress really is—how it affects your mind, body, and behavior.
Recognize your own signs of stress (fatigue, irritability, anxiety, sleeplessness).

🛠️ Practical Coping Tools

Breathing exercises, grounding techniques, or journaling for emotional release.
Time management tips for juggling responsibilities.
How to set healthy boundaries without guilt.
Building self-care habits—without needing extra money or time.

💬 Real-Life Discussions

Everyday challenges—like saying no, asking for help, or handling conflict—are openly talked about.
Participants share what’s worked for them, and learn from each other’s stories.

🌿 Emotional Support

You’re not judged, advised, or “fixed.”
You’re supported, validated, and encouraged.

💡 Life Skills Covered May Include:

  • Self awareness and empathy
  • Communication and assertiveness
  • Problem-solving and decision-making
  • Emotional regulation and self-worth
  • Creative and critical thinking
  • Interpersonal relationship skills
  • Stress management
  • Parenting under stress
  • Work–life balance strategies
